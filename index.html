Assumptions

  • A month is counted as working days (working days per week × 4.345 weeks).
  • Staff cost per scan = monthly salary ÷ working minutes per month × minutes per test — only scanning time is charged to the test.
  • Specialist review: the chosen share of screened patients is expected to be flagged high-risk and reviewed at your cost per review. The ≈25% default reflects a large real-world retinal AI screening deployment (Lee et al., JAMIA 2024).
  • Fixed costs = camera (one-time purchase, or monthly rental if rented) + software license accrued over the chosen horizon. Consumables, marketing and referral revenue are not included.
  • If expected volume exceeds scanning capacity, projections are capped at capacity.
  • All amounts are calculated in USD and shown converted at the rate you set: .

This is a planning estimate, not a quote or financial advice. Figures depend on your actual device pricing, Mediwhale license terms, local reimbursement and staffing costs.
